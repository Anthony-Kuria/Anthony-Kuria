TELECOMMUNICATION.

At Mo-Tech we have a team of experienced engineers and technicians who are extensively trained in RF and transmission networks technologies, therefore responsible for all Radio Frequency Network infrastructure works.
Our expertise in Radio Frequency Infrastructure includes:

  • Technical site survey, design and planning
  • Network drive test and analysis of 2G, 3G, LTE, eLTE, MOS, bench-marking test, Single Site Verification
  • Network Optimization, Monitoring of KPIs, customer complaints optimization, physical network parameter adjustments
  • BTS and RF installation of Antenna, RRU, system module, Microwaves,SMPS, rectifiers
  • BTS commissioning of Ericsson, Huawei and Nokia Flexi equipment
  • In building Base Station (IBS) design, planning, installation and walk test
  • Installation and commissioning alarm system
  • DC upgrade, generator installation and commissioning

Electrical Panel Board Installation

This is also known as a Distribution board “Fuse Board“, “Panel Board” or “Consumer Unit“. This is where the main protective and isolation devices are installed to provide electricity in a safe range to the connected electrical appliances.

We carry out inspection of electrical installations to guarantee quality and certify that the installation meets the required standards and parameters.
